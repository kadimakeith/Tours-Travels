Planning your Perfect Safari

Once you take an African Safari in Kenya, you will agree and appreciate this region is a great destination for not just a safari but some other exciting experiences.
Our tours include adventure safaris from Nairobi to the parks in Kenya like Aberdares. Parks in Tanzania,
Uganda and Rwanda


You can have a relaxing experience by taking a weekend away from the busy schedule of life through our weekend getaways. For the adrenaline junkies
we have several activities that you can take through our sports tours. If you love hiking or mountain climbing, we have itineraries that you can br engage in. For nature lovers, you can take a walk or have a guided tour through the forest or natural trails.


At Madrone Tours and Travel, we believe there is something locally that a person of any interest can do. The young and the old, the introverted
and the extroverted. All you need to do is join us in this adventure as we tembea Kenya. Hakuna Matata!
